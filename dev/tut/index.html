Cervantes

AI-Powered Narrative Formative Assessment

Cervantes transforms traditional exams into Visual Novel adventures where students engage in Socratic dialogues with AI characters. Teachers upload rubrics and receive real-time analytics, while students demonstrate mastery through narrative-driven assessment arcs generated entirely by AI.
