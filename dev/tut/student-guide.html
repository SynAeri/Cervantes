07
Step 7

Socratic Dialogue

After you answer, the AI character responds with Socratic pushback powered by Gemini. This is not grading — it is guided discovery. The character probes your reasoning, surfaces misconceptions, and asks clarifying questions across up to 5 dialogue turns.

The character's emotion shifts based on the quality of your reasoning — encouraging when you show insight, concerned when gaps appear, challenging when pushing you deeper.

Socratic Exchange
You
I think the price rose because demand increased when the new factory opened nearby.
Anya challenging
Interesting. But does a new factory increase demand, or does it affect the supply side? Think about what the factory produces and who buys it.
You
Wait, the factory produces the good — so supply increases, which should lower the price, not raise it...
Anya encouraging
Now you're on the right track! So if the price still rose, what else must have changed?

The character never gives you the answer. It guides you to discover it.

Completion & Progression

Step 8

Scene Completion

When all blocks in a scene have been played through, the system saves your conversation history to the Arc Journal and runs signal extraction on your reasoning quality.

POST /api/arc-journal/append

Signal extraction classifies your demonstrated understanding into one of three levels:

verified mastery auto_fix_high revised_with_scaffolding warning critical_gap

Step 9

Next Scene & Arc Ending

route /arc-ending/[endingId]

After completing a scene, you automatically progress to the next scene — a new character, a new concept, a new angle on the material.

Once all scenes are finished, you arrive at a personalized narrative ending generated by Gemini based on your overall performance. Your ending is unique to the reasoning journey you took.

Visual Novel Script Format

Scenes are authored in a lightweight markup that the VN parser converts into playable blocks:

scene-script.vn
[narration] Scene description text

[character:Anya] [encouraging] Great thinking! But consider...

[player_prompt] What do you think about X?
**A. Option one** – explanation
**B. Option two** – explanation

[freeform] <!-- open-ended response -->
